About Us

Kasama Rides is a trusted destination for cyclists in the Philippines. We offer premium bicycles, high-quality components, and expert services as a result of our shared enthusiasm for cycling and community. Our moniker, "Kasama," which means "companion" in Filipino, represents our dedication to being at your side on every voyage. Whether you're a serious athlete or a recreational rider, we're here to help you succeed with the greatest equipment and services.

Our History

What began as a simple idea among three cycling enthusiasts has grown into a full-service bicycle shop. Through a shared passion, dedication to quality service, and genuine care for our customers, we’ve started building a community of cyclists who share our love for the sport.

What We Offer

  • Premium road bicycles from world-renowned brands
  • High-quality cycling components and accessories
  • Professional bike fitting and maintenance services
